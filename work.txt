Operations & Compliance Technology
(OCT) comprises of functionally aligned development teams who support critical
firm functions such as Legal, Compliance and Operations. OCT develops strategic
solutions that support key business processes, including Settlements,
Confirmations, Regulatory Reporting, Position Keeping, Anti-Money Laundering
and Trade Surveillance.

Global Transaction Reporting
(GTR) Technology serves the global transaction regulatory reporting
requirements of the firm. These requirements span OTC derivatives, listed derivatives
and cash products. The regulations covered are across America (e.g. Dodd-Frank
and CSA), Europe (e.g. MiFID I and EMIR), and Asia (e.g. JFSA and HKMA). The
team are required to work on both meeting the firm’s current regulatory
obligations and on meeting future regulatory obligations (e.g. MiFID II).
